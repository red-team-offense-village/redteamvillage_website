What is Red Team Rumble?

Red Team Rumble is a tournament in which teams compete in a castle vs. castle event.

Teams are tasked with protecting their systems and services while identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities in other teams' castles.

By defending their own services and attacking opponents' systems, teams get points for controlling services and flags.

When is Red Team Rumble Taking Place?

Saturday, Aug 10, 2024 10:00am-6:00pm

Where Will Red Team Rumble Be Held?

The event will be held at the Red Team Village Competition Area at DefCon 32 

What Do I Win?

We have prizes for the first-place team! The specifics are still a secret, though. You'll find out more at Defcon!

Event Structure

Teams will defend their "Castle," a virtual environment comprising several systems and services (Windows and Linux systems will be included).

Each castle has exposed services and exploitable vulnerabilities, along with a few hidden extras.

Your Team's Mission!

Identify and patch vulnerabilities within your "Castle" to protect your systems while keeping critical services online and infiltrating other team castles.

Earn points by safeguarding your Castle, securing objectives, or completing tasks within other teams' Castles.

Remember, time is of the essence! Each round lasts just two hours, so every second counts!

How Do I Participate?

If you are interested in participating, please click the Sign-Up Button at the top of the page. We'll then contact 8 teams that we think would be a good fit for this trial event.

We'll have space for up to 8 players on a team to participate in the competition area. Please provide a team name and details about your team and why you'd like to be invited to this event.

Important Things to Note:

Your team must attend the event at the Las Vegas Convention Center, which is located in the competition area.

You must bring your own equipment (laptops, power, etc.) to access the competition environment. We will have a table for your team to use and internet access, but we will not provide laptops or other equipment.
