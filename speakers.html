Speakers and Trainers

Paul Asadoorian is the Founder and CEO of Security Weekly, a security podcast network providing free security information to the community and security market validation to a wide variety of security companies

Evan Anderson is the Director of Offensive Security at Randori.

Phillip Wylie - Penetration Tester, Adjunct Instructor, The Pwn School Project Founder, Uncommon Journey Podcast Co-host.

Krassimir Tzvetanov focuses on Threat Intelligence, Operational Security and Counterintelligence techniques (in the cyber domain). 

Jorge Orchilles is the Chief Technology Officer at SCYTHE. Instructor, Author, and C2 Matrix Project Lead.

Chloé Messdaghi is an Ethical Hacker Advocate & Humanitarian. She is also the VP of Strategy at Point3 Security, Inc.
